At Starlight Events, we help manage your big ideas and your smallest concerns to ensure you are stress-free and enjoying every moment. For more than a decade, we’ve been working with clients to create exceptional experiences for their nonprofit fundraiser or social gathering.

Meet The Owner

Over the last decade, Kristin has planned and executed hundreds of events ranging from large-scale conventions to themed holiday parties to intimate networking dinners and everything in between. Her creativity, attention to detail, organizational skills and passion for planning ensures the success of every celebration she’s a part of.

Kristin began her career working at the Sheraton Boston Hotel, the largest hotel in Boston, where she realized her love for planning, logistics, and all the little details that make a spectacular event. From there, she was recruited by the Director of Employee Programs at Partners HealthCare, where she planned corporate and employee recognition events for almost ten years.
